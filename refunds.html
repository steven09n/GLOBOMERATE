Refund Policy

Globomerate, LLC

All services provided by Globomerate, LLC are rendered on a non-refundable basis. As a professional service provider, our time, expertise, and availability are committed in advance upon engagement. This policy ensures fairness to all clients and supports operational integrity.

Finality of Payment

All payments—whether for retainers, fixed-fee projects, or hourly consultations—are final once the engagement begins. Refunds will not be granted in the following circumstances:

  • Client changes mind or shifts project priorities
  • Delays or issues caused by the client (e.g., missing documents, non-responsiveness)
  • Cancellation of the project after work has started
  • Client does not utilize scheduled hours or sessions

Note: Each engagement reserves time and resources that cannot be reassigned once committed.

Cancellation Terms

  • Hourly or Scheduled Sessions: Clients may reschedule with a minimum of 48 hours’ notice. Missed sessions or cancellations within 48 hours are deducted from total hours booked.
  • Retainer or Fixed-Fee Packages: Once active, these are non-transferable and time-sensitive. Any unused time will not be refunded, extended, or banked unless explicitly agreed in writing prior to engagement.

Discretionary Adjustments

In exceptional circumstances, and solely at our discretion, a credit toward future services may be issued. This requires timely communication and documented justification.

All discretionary credits are reviewed case-by-case and must be formally requested in writing.

Dispute Resolution

Clients are encouraged to raise concerns as soon as they arise by email. We will make every effort to resolve disputes professionally and fairly.

However, our no-refund policy remains applicable once service delivery has commenced. This clarity ensures mutual understanding and accountability throughout the engagement.
