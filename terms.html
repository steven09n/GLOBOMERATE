Business Engagement Terms

Globomerate, LLC

Globomerate, LLC offers specialized business consulting, international business services, and worldwide support tailored to professionals, entrepreneurs, and organizations operating across borders. Each engagement is structured according to the unique needs of the client and defined through a clear agreement before services begin.

Engagement Structure

We offer the following service formats:

  • Monthly Advisory Retainers: For clients needing ongoing support and guidance on operations, international transactions, or compliance.
  • Fixed-Fee Packages: Custom packages for project-based work, such as entity structure, market development, document preparation, or due diligence.
  • Hourly Consulting: Ideal for one-off sessions, strategic reviews, or expert insight.

Every project begins with a discovery consultation. Scope, pricing, and deliverables are clearly outlined in a client agreement or invoice prior to work commencement.

Service Delivery Options

  • Remote engagements: Conducted via phone, video conference, email, and secure document portals.
  • In-person sessions: Available to clients within our service area or by travel arrangement. Travel costs and per diem expenses are discussed and agreed upon in writing prior to scheduling.

We reserve the right to decline in-person meetings when remote delivery is more appropriate for the scope or location.

Communication & Timeline

Business hours are typically Monday to Friday, 9 AM–5 PM EST. Clients are expected to respond to requests for information in a timely manner to avoid delays.

Turnaround times vary depending on the type of engagement, but typical timelines are communicated during the onboarding process. Delays caused by client-side issues (missing documents, lack of payment, or no communication) will not be attributed to the consultant.

Agreement Confirmation

All services are considered active once:

  • A written agreement or invoice has been acknowledged via email or signature
  • Payment has been received or a scheduled retainer has begun

We do not begin work on verbal instructions or unsigned proposals.

Scope Adjustments

If the scope of work changes after an agreement is confirmed, a new quote or service addendum will be issued. Work outside the scope will not be performed without prior written approval.

Suspension or Termination

Services may be paused or terminated if the client becomes unresponsive, fails to provide critical documentation, or otherwise breaches terms of the agreement. In such cases, any unused service time may be forfeited unless an exception is granted at our discretion.

Our aim is to maintain a respectful, professional partnership at every stage of the engagement.
