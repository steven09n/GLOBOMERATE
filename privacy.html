Global Privacy Practices

Globomerate, LLC

At Globomerate, LLC, safeguarding client confidentiality is at the core of every service we offer. Serving a diverse clientele across national and international borders, we adhere to rigorous privacy protocols that comply with applicable regional and global data protection laws.

Information We Collect

As part of our client service process, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Business and personal contact details
  • Corporate documentation and regulatory submissions
  • Financial and operational records necessary for consulting services
  • Communications such as emails, meeting notes, and client directives

We do not collect or retain any payment card information. All financial transactions are conducted through secure, third-party platforms that meet industry compliance standards.

How We Use Client Information

Information shared with us is used exclusively for:

  • Fulfilling the scope of contracted services
  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
  • Maintaining accurate internal documentation

We do not rent, sell, or share client data with external parties for advertising, promotional, or unrelated commercial purposes.

Data Protection & Access Controls

Globomerate implements strong administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect all client data, including:

  • Encrypted digital storage solutions (e.g., Google Workspace, Dropbox Business)
  • Role-based access permissions within our team
  • Monitoring and audit logs when necessary

Client data is never shared externally without explicit written authorization, unless required by law or regulatory obligation.

International Compliance & Sector-Specific Standards

We comply with global data privacy laws and tailor our privacy practices to reflect:

  • Confidentiality terms per client agreements
  • U.S. federal and state data regulations
  • European Union data protection standards (GDPR and related frameworks)

For clients in regulated industries (e.g., finance, healthcare, education), we offer enhanced privacy terms, including NDAs and contractual clauses, at onboarding or upon request.

Clients must disclose any specific privacy or regulatory needs prior to project initiation.

Data Retention & Deletion

Client data is retained only for as long as is necessary to:

  • Complete the service
  • Comply with legal and contractual obligations
  • Fulfill terms agreed upon with the client

Upon project completion or contract termination, clients may request:

  • Return of their data
  • Permanent deletion of stored records
  • Written confirmation of data handling actions

All such requests must be formally submitted in writing.
